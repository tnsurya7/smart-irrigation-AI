# Team Member Images

Place the following team member images in this directory:

- `moni.png` - MONIKA M (Frontend Development & UI/UX)
- `surya.png` - SURYA KUMAR M (Backend Development & ML)  
- `karan.png` - KARAN M (IoT Integration & Hardware)

## Image Requirements

- **Format**: PNG or JPG
- **Size**: 400x400 pixels (square)
- **Background**: Professional headshot or avatar
- **Quality**: High resolution for crisp display

## Usage

These images will be displayed in the Project Landing page team section with hover effects and professional styling suitable for academic presentation.

## Fallback

If images are not available, the system will display professional avatar placeholders with appropriate icons.
